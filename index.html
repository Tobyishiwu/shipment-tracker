Support & Policy

What is "GoFaster" service? +
GoFaster is our priority interstate delivery service. It guarantees that shipments within Nigeria reach their destination within 24 to 48 hours, perfect for urgent business documents and time-critical items.
How do I ship from USA/UK/China to Nigeria? +
Through the GIGGo app or our web dashboard, you can get a dedicated overseas address. Once your items arrive at our global hub (Houston, London, or Guangzhou), we consolidate and ship them directly to your doorstep in Nigeria or Ghana.
